1. Open moltstream.

## 📡 Tips for Smooth Streaming

- Use a wired internet connection if possible for better speed and stability.  
- Close other programs that use the internet heavily while streaming.  
- Check your stream’s video and audio before going live to ensure quality.  
- Adjust bitrate settings in moltstream if your connection is slow. Lower bitrate reduces video quality but prevents lag.

---

## 🔧 Troubleshooting Common Issues

- **Stream is delayed or lagging:** Try lowering the resolution or bitrate in the settings. Also, check your internet speed.  
- **Cannot find Stream Key:** Go to your streaming platform's dashboard and look for the stream key section (usually under settings or channel).  
- **App won’t start:** Make sure Windows is up to date. Try restarting your PC and running moltstream as an administrator.  
- **Video source not showing:** Check if your camera or screen capture is connected and allowed by the app.
